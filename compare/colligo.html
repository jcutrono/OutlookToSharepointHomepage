FileMy.Email vs Colligo Email Manager

Colligo Email Manager for Microsoft 365 has the most overlapping feature set with FileMy.Email of any tool we compare against — and meaningfully different pricing. Every claim about Colligo below is sourced to their published documentation.

Last verified: May 2026

Starting at $10/mo for 3 users. See pricing

Quick verdict

Colligo Email Manager for M365 is a mature email-filing tool with strong compliance and records-management focus. They publish three tiers — Basic at $12/user/month and Premium at $22/user/month, both billed annually[1]. Their published feature set covers one-click filing, drag-and-drop, suggestive filing locations, Auto-File on a schedule, Send & file, AI Summary, and retention labels[2]. Built for regulated industries: Financial Services, Government, Legal, Healthcare, AEC[3].

FileMy.Email covers a similar core: smart folder suggestions, Save on Send, drag-and-drop is replaced by a learning-driven folder picker, batch inbox cleanup, auto-archive while away. $10/month for 3 users, monthly billing, no annual contract.

The honest tradeoff: if regulatory features (retention labels applied at save, AI Summary, deeper compliance integrations) are a hard requirement, Colligo earned its enterprise position. If you want the core filing workflow at ≈1/4 to 1/8 the per-user cost without an annual contract, FileMy.Email fits.

When each one is the right pick

Pick Colligo when

  • Compliance is core: you need retention labels applied automatically on save[2]
  • You operate in a regulated industry — Financial Services, Government, Legal, Healthcare[3]
  • Drag-and-drop into a folder tree is a workflow your team prefers[2]
  • You want AI Summary previews of email content[2]
  • Annual procurement and a $144+/user/year price point are acceptable[1]

Pick FileMy.Email when

  • You want monthly billing and the ability to scale seats up or down anytime
  • The Basic tier price ($144/user/year) is more than your team can justify
  • Auto-archive while away matches your workflow (out of office filing for known senders)
  • You want EML or PDF format choice for the saved record
  • You're 3–100 users and don't need enterprise compliance integrations
