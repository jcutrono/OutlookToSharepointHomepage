How It Works

Three steps. No training required.

1

Open or Compose

The add-in panel appears alongside your message — whether you're reading mail or writing it.

2

Pick a Folder

Accept the smart suggestion, or browse and search your SharePoint libraries or OneDrive.

3

Save or Send

Click Save, or just hit Send with Save on Send turned on. The email and attachments are filed instantly with metadata columns filled in.

What Makes This Different

Built for the way you actually work.

Gets smarter the more you use it — both directions

Most tools make you set up rules manually. This one learns your habits automatically — for incoming mail and outgoing mail — and gets better over time. The folder you use for messages from a client also surfaces when you write to them.

Files your sent mail too

Turn on Save on Send while composing and the email files itself the second you hit Send. No "remember to save it later," no double-handling — outgoing mail lands in the right folder automatically.

Batch-process your entire inbox

Stop filing one email at a time. Scan your inbox, preview where everything goes, and save it all in one click.

Metadata fills itself in

SharePoint column mapping means you never have to manually type in the sender, date, or subject. It's done for you.

Keeps working when you're not

Flip on Auto-Archive before you head out. Matching mail files itself while you're away — only the senders you've already mapped, never anything else.

Private by design

Your emails go directly to your SharePoint or OneDrive. We never read, store, or route email content through our servers. No middleware, no middleman.
