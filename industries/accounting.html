Busy season makes the email problem worse

Tax season alone produces a year's worth of email volume in four months. Every client sends W-2s, 1099s, K-1s, brokerage statements, "one quick question" replies, and corrections. The official process is to file all of it to the client folder. The actual process:

  • Staff download attachments to the desktop, rename them, then upload to SharePoint
  • Sent emails — confirmations, tax return PDFs, e-file acknowledgments — never make it to the client folder
  • Document portal isn't used for ad-hoc client sends, so half the docs never enter the system
  • By April 16, the partner can't find the email where the client confirmed the home office deduction
  • Next year's preparer starts from scratch because last year's correspondence is buried

This add-in turns email filing into a one-click step inside Outlook. Smart suggestions learn each client's folder. Save on Send catches outgoing mail automatically. Attachment names follow your firm's convention every time.

Frequently asked questions

Does this work alongside our document portal?

Yes. Document portals (SmartVault, Canopy, ShareFile) handle client-facing document exchange. They don't solve the everyday email that piles up in Outlook. This add-in routes that email and the one-off PDFs into SharePoint without retraining clients to use the portal for every send.

Can I file by client and engagement year?

Yes. The add-in browses any SharePoint folder structure. Client / Year / Engagement is a common layout. Save once, and the add-in suggests it for that client's future mail.

How is confidential client data handled?

Files transfer directly from Outlook to your SharePoint using your firm's Microsoft 365 credentials. Email content is never read, stored, or proxied through our servers.

Can the whole tax team use it during busy season?

Yes. $10/month for 3 users plus $3 per additional user, billed monthly with no annual commitment. Add seasonal staff for busy season and remove after.

Will it save attachments with consistent file names?

Yes. Configure a naming template once. Every saved attachment follows it firm-wide.

Does it work with shared and delegate mailboxes?

Yes. Works in any mailbox the user has access to in Outlook, including admin@ shared mailboxes that intake client documents.
