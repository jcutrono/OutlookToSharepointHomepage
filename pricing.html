How Subscriptions Work

When you subscribe, you'll check out through Stripe. The base plan covers 3 users. If you need more seats, increase the quantity during checkout to add additional users.

After subscribing, you can manage everything through the Stripe billing portal — add or remove seats, update your payment method, or cancel your plan.

Manage Subscription

Changes are prorated — if you add users mid-cycle, you only pay for the remaining days. If you remove users, you'll receive a credit toward your next invoice.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel through the billing portal and your access continues until the end of your current billing period.

How do I add more users?

Log into the billing portal and update your subscription quantity. The change takes effect immediately.

Are charges prorated?

Yes. You're only charged for what you use. Adding users mid-cycle bills only for the remaining days; removing users gives you a credit.

What counts as a "user"?

Each person who uses the add-in occupies one seat. Seats are claimed automatically on first use. Tenant admins can remove users from seats in the add-in's settings to free up space.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit and debit cards through Stripe. We never see or store your card details.
