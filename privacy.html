Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

This policy explains how FileMy.Email handles personal data across two things: our website at filemy.email, and the Outlook add-in ("the Add-in") that saves your emails and attachments to SharePoint or OneDrive. The two are very different in what they touch, so we describe them separately below.

Who We Are

FileMy.Email is operated by Practical Software Solutions LLC ("we", "us", "our"), a company based in the United States. For any privacy question, or to exercise your rights, contact us at support@filemy.email.

For visitors and customers in the UK or European Economic Area (EEA), we act as the data controller for personal data collected through our website, and as a data processor acting on your organization's instructions for the limited data the Add-in handles on your behalf.

Part A — The Website (filemy.email)

Analytics

When you visit our website, we use Google Analytics 4 and Google Tag Manager to understand how the site is used (which pages are visited, how visitors arrive, and general usage trends). This involves data such as your IP address, device and browser type, approximate location (derived from IP), pages viewed, and referring website. This data is collected through cookies (see the table below) and is processed by Google on our behalf.

Payments

When you purchase a subscription, checkout is handled by Stripe. You enter your payment details directly with Stripe — we never see or store your full card number. Stripe processes your name, email, billing details, and payment information to complete the transaction and to prevent fraud. See Stripe's privacy policy at stripe.com/privacy.

Support

If you email us, we receive your email address and whatever you include in your message, and we keep that correspondence so we can help you and maintain a support record.

Part B — The Outlook Add-in

The Add-in is designed to keep your email data private. It reads only what it needs to file a message, and it does not route your email content through our servers.

What the Add-in Accesses

When you choose to save an email, the Add-in reads the following metadata from the selected message:

  • Sender name and email address
  • Subject line
  • Date sent
  • Attachment file names

This is used solely to suggest a destination folder and to name the saved file according to your preferences.

What the Add-in Stores

  • Folder mapping preferences — rules you create to route emails to specific SharePoint or OneDrive folders
  • File naming settings — your chosen templates for naming saved files

This data is stored within your Microsoft 365 environment and is associated with your account.

What the Add-in Does NOT Store

  • Email content or message bodies
  • Attachment file contents
  • Passwords or authentication tokens
  • Copies of any files saved to SharePoint or OneDrive

How the Add-in Transfers Your Email Data

When you save an email or attachment, the file is transferred directly from Outlook to your SharePoint site or OneDrive over encrypted connections. Your email content and files are not sent to, routed through, or stored on FileMy.Email servers. The Add-in does not share your email content with any analytics provider or advertising network.

Authentication

The Add-in uses your organization's existing Microsoft 365 authentication (OAuth 2.0). We do not handle, see, or store your password.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the service providers needed to run FileMy.Email:

  • Google — website analytics (privacy policy)
  • Stripe — payment processing (privacy policy)
  • Microsoft — you connect your own Microsoft 365 tenant; your email and files stay within your Microsoft environment

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

International Data Transfers

We are based in the United States, and our service providers (including Google and Stripe) process data in the United States and other countries. If you are in the UK or EEA, your personal data will be transferred to the United States. Where required, these transfers rely on appropriate safeguards such as the EU–US and UK Data Privacy Framework and/or Standard Contractual Clauses (with the UK Addendum / IDTA) put in place by these providers.

Data Retention

We keep website analytics data for up to 26 months, payment records for as long as required for tax and accounting law, and support correspondence for as long as needed to assist you. Add-in preferences are kept in your Microsoft 365 environment until you remove them or uninstall the Add-in.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@filemy.email. We do not sell your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address above.

Security

All data travels over secure, encrypted (HTTPS/TLS) connections. The Add-in relies on Microsoft 365 OAuth and never stores your password. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

Children's Privacy

FileMy.Email is a business tool and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16.
