Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Save to SharePoint ("the Add-in") is an Outlook add-in that helps you save emails and attachments to SharePoint. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the Add-in accesses, how it is used, and what is stored.

What We Access

When you choose to save an email, the Add-in reads the following metadata from the currently selected message:

  • Sender name and email address
  • Subject line
  • Date sent
  • Attachment file names

This information is used solely to suggest a destination folder and to name the saved file according to your preferences.

What We Store

The Add-in stores only:

  • Folder mapping preferences — rules you create to route emails to specific SharePoint folders
  • File naming settings — your chosen templates for naming saved files

This data is stored within your Microsoft 365 environment and is associated with your account.

What We Do NOT Store

  • Email content or message bodies
  • Attachment file contents
  • Passwords or authentication tokens
  • Copies of any files saved to SharePoint

Data Transfer

When you save an email or attachment, the file is transferred directly to your SharePoint site over encrypted connections. No data is sent to or stored on any third-party servers outside of Microsoft 365.

Authentication

The Add-in uses your organization's existing Microsoft 365 authentication (OAuth 2.0). We do not handle, see, or store your password.

Third-Party Services

The Add-in does not share data with any third-party services, analytics providers, or advertising networks.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the Add-in after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit filemy.email or contact support.
